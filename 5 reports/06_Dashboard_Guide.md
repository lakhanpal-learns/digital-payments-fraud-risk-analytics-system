# Dashboard Guide

## Overview

The Digital Payments & Fraud Risk Analytics System consists of four interactive Power BI dashboards. Each dashboard is designed for a specific business function and provides actionable insights for different stakeholders.

---

# 1. Executive Dashboard

## Purpose

Provides a high-level overview of the digital payment ecosystem and overall business performance.

### Intended Audience

* Executive Leadership
* Business Managers
* Product Managers

### KPIs

* Total Transactions
* Total Payment Volume
* Success Rate
* Failure Rate
* Average Latency

### Visualizations

* Transaction Volume Trend
* Payment Method Usage
* Transactions by City

### Business Questions Answered

* How healthy is the payment ecosystem?
* How many transactions are processed?
* Which payment methods are most popular?
* Which cities generate the highest transaction volume?

---

# 2. Fraud Risk Analytics Dashboard

## Purpose

Monitors fraud activity, financial exposure, and high-risk transactions.

### Intended Audience

* Fraud Analysts
* Risk Management Team
* Compliance Team

### KPIs

* Fraud Rate
* Fraud Transactions
* Fraud Exposure Amount
* High-Risk Transactions

### Visualizations

* Fraud Transactions by City
* Fraud Transactions by Payment Method
* Fraud Transactions by Day
* Top High-Risk Transactions

### Business Questions Answered

* Where is fraud occurring?
* Which payment methods have higher fraud activity?
* How much financial exposure exists?
* Which transactions require immediate investigation?

---

# 3. Payment Operations Dashboard

## Purpose

Monitors payment performance, operational efficiency, and transaction reliability.

### Intended Audience

* Payment Operations Team
* Platform Reliability Team
* Business Operations Team

### KPIs

* Average Latency
* Failure Rate
* Failed Transactions
* Retry Rate

### Visualizations

* Latency by Payment Method
* Failure Reasons
* Transaction Volume by Hour
* Merchant Failure Rate
* Retry Attempts Distribution

### Business Questions Answered

* Why are transactions failing?
* Which payment channels experience higher latency?
* Which merchants have the highest failure rates?
* How frequently are transactions retried?

---

# 4. Merchant & Customer Insights Dashboard

## Purpose

Analyzes merchant performance, customer behavior, and revenue contribution.

### Intended Audience

* Merchant Success Team
* Business Analysts
* Product Managers

### KPIs

* Total Merchants
* Total Customers
* Average Transaction Value
* Total Revenue

### Visualizations

* Top Merchants by Revenue
* Transactions by City
* Transaction Share by Payment Method
* Revenue by Payment Method

### Business Questions Answered

* Which merchants generate the highest revenue?
* Which cities contribute the most transactions?
* Which payment methods dominate transaction volume?
* What is the average customer transaction value?

---

# Conclusion

Together, these dashboards provide a comprehensive view of the digital payment ecosystem, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify fraud risks, improve operational efficiency, and make data-driven decisions.
