# Business Problem

## Background

Digital payment platforms process millions of transactions every day through channels such as UPI, credit cards, debit cards, wallets, and net banking. As transaction volumes continue to grow, financial institutions must ensure that payments are processed reliably, efficiently, and securely.

Even a small increase in transaction failures or fraudulent activities can result in financial losses, customer dissatisfaction, operational inefficiencies, and reputational damage.

---

# Problem Statement

FinTech organizations require a centralized analytics solution capable of monitoring payment operations, detecting fraudulent activities, evaluating merchant performance, and analyzing customer transaction behavior.

Without continuous monitoring, organizations face challenges such as:

* Increasing payment failure rates
* High transaction processing latency
* Fraudulent payment attempts
* Poor merchant performance visibility
* Limited customer transaction insights
* Delayed operational decision-making

---

# Business Challenges

### Payment Operations

* Monitoring payment success and failure rates
* Identifying the leading causes of transaction failures
* Measuring payment processing latency
* Understanding retry behavior during failed transactions

---

### Fraud Risk Management

* Detecting suspicious transactions
* Monitoring fraud trends across cities and payment methods
* Prioritizing high-risk transactions for investigation
* Estimating financial exposure caused by fraudulent activities

---

### Merchant Performance

* Identifying top revenue-generating merchants
* Measuring merchant failure rates
* Understanding merchant contribution to transaction volume

---

### Customer Analytics

* Measuring customer transaction activity
* Understanding payment method preferences
* Identifying high-value customers

---

# Project Solution

This project provides a complete analytics solution by integrating:

* Synthetic transaction generation using Python
* PostgreSQL database for structured storage
* SQL-based business analytics
* Interactive Power BI dashboards
* Business KPIs for operational and fraud monitoring

The solution enables business users to monitor payment ecosystem health, identify operational bottlenecks, detect fraud risks, and generate actionable business insights.
