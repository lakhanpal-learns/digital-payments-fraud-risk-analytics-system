# Digital Payments & Fraud Risk Analytics System

## Final Project Report

---

# Executive Summary

The Digital Payments & Fraud Risk Analytics System is an end-to-end fintech analytics solution designed to simulate a modern digital payment ecosystem. The project integrates synthetic data generation, relational database management, SQL analytics, and interactive Power BI dashboards to monitor payment operations, detect fraud, evaluate merchant performance, and analyze customer transaction behavior.

The solution demonstrates the complete analytics lifecycle—from data generation and storage to business intelligence and decision support.

---

# Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project were:

* Develop a realistic digital payment transaction dataset.
* Store transaction data in PostgreSQL.
* Perform business analytics using SQL.
* Design interactive Power BI dashboards.
* Monitor payment operations and fraud risks.
* Generate business insights to support decision-making.

# Dashboards Developed

### Executive Dashboard

Purpose:

Provide an overall view of payment ecosystem performance.

KPIs:

* Total Transactions
* Total Payment Volume
* Success Rate
* Failure Rate
* Average Latency

---

### Fraud Risk Analytics Dashboard

Purpose:

Monitor fraud exposure and high-risk transactions.

KPIs:

* Fraud Rate
* Fraud Transactions
* Fraud Exposure Amount
* High-Risk Transactions

---

### Payment Operations Dashboard

Purpose:

Evaluate operational efficiency and payment reliability.

KPIs:

* Average Latency
* Retry Rate
* Failed Transactions
* Merchant Failure Rate

---

### Merchant & Customer Insights Dashboard

Purpose:

Analyze merchant performance and customer transaction behavior.

KPIs:

* Total Merchants
* Total Customers
* Average Transaction Value
* Total Revenue

---

# Major Business Insights

* The payment ecosystem maintained a high transaction success rate.
* UPI was the dominant payment channel by transaction volume.
* Fraud activity was concentrated in selected cities and payment methods.
* Payment retries highlighted customer payment friction.
* Certain merchants contributed significantly to total revenue.
* Payment latency varied across payment channels.

---

# Business Recommendations

* Continuously monitor payment success and latency.
* Prioritize fraud monitoring for high-risk regions and transactions.
* Improve payment retry mechanisms to enhance customer experience.
* Optimize payment routing for slower payment methods.
* Strengthen operational monitoring for high-value merchants.
* Use KPI-driven dashboards for proactive business decisions.
