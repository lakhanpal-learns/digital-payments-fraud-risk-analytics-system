# KPI Definitions

## Overview

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are quantitative metrics used to evaluate the performance, reliability, and security of the digital payment ecosystem. These KPIs enable stakeholders to monitor payment operations, detect fraud, assess merchant performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

# Executive KPIs

| KPI                  | Definition                                        | Business Importance                                |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Total Transactions   | Total number of payment transactions processed    | Measures overall transaction activity              |
| Total Payment Volume | Total monetary value of all transactions          | Indicates the scale of business processed          |
| Success Rate         | Percentage of successfully completed transactions | Measures payment system reliability                |
| Failure Rate         | Percentage of failed transactions                 | Identifies operational issues and payment friction |
| Average Latency      | Average transaction processing time (ms)          | Measures payment processing efficiency             |

---

# Fraud Risk KPIs

| KPI                    | Definition                                    | Business Importance                         |
| ---------------------- | --------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Fraud Transactions     | Total number of fraudulent transactions       | Measures fraud activity                     |
| Fraud Rate             | Percentage of fraudulent transactions         | Evaluates fraud exposure                    |
| Fraud Exposure Amount  | Total transaction value associated with fraud | Estimates potential financial loss          |
| High Risk Transactions | Number of transactions with high risk scores  | Supports fraud investigation prioritization |

---

## Payment Operations KPIs

| KPI                                | Definition                                              | Business Importance                                      |
|------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------|
| Retry Rate                         | Percentage of transactions requiring one or more retry attempts | Indicates payment friction and customer payment experience |
| Failed Transactions                | Total number of failed transactions                     | Measures operational stability and payment reliability   |
| Merchant Failure Rate              | Percentage of failed transactions for each merchant     | Identifies merchants experiencing payment processing issues |
| Average Latency by Payment Method  | Average processing time for each payment channel        | Evaluates payment channel performance and operational efficiency |

# Merchant & Customer KPIs

| KPI                       | Definition                                        | Business Importance                 |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Total Merchants           | Number of unique merchants                        | Measures merchant ecosystem size    |
| Total Customers           | Number of unique customers                        | Indicates customer base size        |
| Average Transaction Value | Average value per transaction                     | Reflects customer spending behavior |
| Revenue by Merchant       | Total payment volume processed by each merchant   | Identifies high-value merchants     |
| Transactions by City      | Number of transactions originating from each city | Supports geographic market analysis |

# Business Value

These KPIs enable organizations to:

* Monitor payment ecosystem health
* Identify operational bottlenecks
* Detect fraud trends
* Evaluate merchant performance
* Analyze customer transaction behavior
* Improve payment reliability
* Support strategic business decisions
